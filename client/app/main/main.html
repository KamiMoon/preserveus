Mission

Preserve US allows people from all over to connect and provide tools and resources that will allow key culture sites to be preserved in the United States. We are dedicated to saving the rich history of our collective communities and fight to keep these buildings as living artifacts that will be around for generations to come.

Operations

Preserve US is a full service operation that specializes in Development & Restoration. Our finance team operates both debt & equity for our properties, including private equity opportunities for investors. Our development team operates all aspects of construction, including additions, new construction, remodel, security feature, and technical development. Our property management team operates all daily on-goings of the buildings, including maintenance, leasing, and front office. We provide full service for investors from purchase, operation, and sale of all properties.

Values

Preserve US was founded because of our love of great properties that have a rich history to them. Our company strives to bring historic buildings back to their original grandeur and preserving the living culture within. Our renovation projects keep the original feel of the property, while adding foundational and technological improvements that allow full operation for ages to come.

We value allowing the opportunity for every citizen to make a difference in their community by providing resources to ensure properties they love are kept in place and in good hands. Every single person makes and impact on the environment around them, and we believe that all citizens should be able to easily donate to physical projects near and dear to them.
